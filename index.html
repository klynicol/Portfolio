pizzachronicle.com

A personal project of mine. Pizza Chronicle is a single-page, user-based web application focused on ratings for frozen pizza and eventually restaurants. Login/Register and start posting ratings and reviews or just sit back and watch your client automatically display others comments and reviews. Written with mainly Typescript using Meteor.js, Angular 4, Node.js, MongoDB, Handlebars and Bootstrap by yours truly. UX and UI also designed by me. Credit for the logo and illustrations goes to my lovely girlfriend Tiffany (creatiffity.me).

maryannkuchera.com

A portfolio website for MaryAnn Kuchera, a massage therapist and artist living in Seattle. The challenge; She had a LOT of book reviews, pictures and other material that need to be organized and accessible. Her audience is mainly older people, thus I wanted to keep the site super minimal and easy to navigate. Designed and build from scratch by me using nothing but HTML, CSS and Javascript (Bootstrap, Jquery + plugins). Credit for the logo and illustrations goes to my lovely girlfriend Tiffany (creatiffity.me).

aceseattle.com

Luis Aceves owns a custodial service in Seattle and needed a website to present his brand with in a fresh professional manner. I built him a single page standalone site with Bootstrap. He was looking to take his business to the next level and I believe his website will be a great launching point for it. Credit for the logo and icons goes to my lovely girlfriend Tiffany (creatiffity.me).
